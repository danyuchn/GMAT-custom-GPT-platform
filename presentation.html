Phase 1: MVP Launch – Taiwan GMAT Market

Net Profit Target: $2,000

Goals & Market

  • Goal: Validate GMAT market with a working MVP
  • Market: 3,000 GMAT test-takers in Taiwan
  • Target Users: 630 (21% conversion)
  • Timeline: 3 months (4 weeks dev + 8 weeks promotion)

Core Features

  • Multilingual UI (Traditional Chinese & English)
  • AI-based Q&A (Logic, Math, Reading)
  • Email/password login with usage tracking
  • Quota system: 50 free, 200 uses @ $9.99/mo
  • Redeemable code plan: $4.99/150 turns
